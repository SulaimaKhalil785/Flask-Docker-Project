Our Philosophy

Cooking with fire,
serving with soul.

At Ember, we believe that the oldest cooking element — fire — is also the most honest. Every dish begins with intention: a live flame, seasonal ingredients sourced within 80 miles, and decades of craft.

Chef Ariana Volkov leads our kitchen with a philosophy rooted in restraint and precision. Nothing is superfluous. Everything on the plate has a reason to exist.
